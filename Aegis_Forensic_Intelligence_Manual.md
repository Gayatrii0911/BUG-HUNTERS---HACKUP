## 🔬 The Risk Scoring Formula

Aegis calculates the **Neural Risk Index (NRI)** using a 4-vector weighted fusion:

$$NRI = (0.35 * ML) + (0.30 * GR) + (0.25 * BP) + (0.10 * DI)$$

- **ML (Machine Learning - 35%)**: Isolation Forest anomaly score (checks for volume/time spikes).
- **GR (Graph Relationship - 30%)**: Distance to known fraud nodes (Money Mule Hubs).
- **BP (Behavioral Profile - 25%)**: Checks for history-breaking actions (e.g., first-time large transfer to new receiver).
- **DI (Device Intelligence - 10%)**: Checks for hardware fingerprint mismatch (new device + high amount).
